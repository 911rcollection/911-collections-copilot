SMS Privacy Policy

911 Restoration · Effective June 19, 2026

This policy explains how 911 Restoration handles the personal information collected through its SMS (text message) program for restoration customers. It supplements 911 Restoration's general Privacy Policy.

What we collect

When you opt in to our SMS program we collect your mobile phone number, your consent record (date/time and selection), and information needed to send relevant service messages about your restoration job and insurance billing (such as job number, claim number, and billing status).

How we use it

We use this information only to operate this messaging program — to send you account, claim, and billing-status notifications and service updates regarding your restoration job. These are transactional messages and are not marketing.

No sharing of mobile information. 911 Restoration does not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or your SMS opt-in/consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is used solely to deliver the service messages you requested. No mobile information is shared with third parties for their own marketing.

Service providers

We use trusted technology providers (for example, our messaging carrier and software platforms) strictly to deliver these messages and operate the program on our behalf. They are not permitted to use your mobile information for any other purpose.

Your choices

Consent is optional and is not a condition of receiving any 911 Restoration service. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message; reply HELP for help. You may also call 971-533-0214 or email officeassistant@911restoration.com.

Message frequency & cost

Message frequency varies based on activity on your job, claim, and billing. Message and data rates may apply, charged by your mobile carrier.

Data retention

We retain your mobile number and consent record for as long as needed to operate the program and to meet legal and recordkeeping requirements, after which it is deleted or de-identified.

Contact
