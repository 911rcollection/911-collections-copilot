SMS Messaging Terms & Conditions

911 Restoration · Effective June 18, 2026

Program

911 Restoration operates an SMS program that sends account, claim, and billing-status notifications and service updates to customers regarding their water, fire, and mold restoration jobs and the associated insurance billing (for example: claim review updates, payment-forwarding reminders, and past-due payment notices). These are transactional, service-related messages — not marketing.

Consent

You opt in to this SMS program through 911 Restoration's onboarding consent form. Consent is not a condition of purchasing or receiving any 911 Restoration service. You may receive service-related communication by other channels even if you do not opt in to texts.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the activity on your job, claim, and billing.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply — these are charged by your mobile carrier, not by 911 Restoration.

How to Opt Out or Get Help

To stop messages at any time, reply STOP to any text message. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts.

For help, reply HELP to any text message, call us at 971-533-0214, or email officeassistant@911restoration.com.

Privacy & Data Sharing

Your mobile number and SMS consent are used only to operate this 911 Restoration messaging program. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number or SMS opt-in with any third parties, and we do not use this program for marketing. Your information is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Supported Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Changes

We may update these SMS terms from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
