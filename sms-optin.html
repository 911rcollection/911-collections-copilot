Who we are

911 Restoration is a water, fire, and mold property-restoration company serving the Portland, OR and Vancouver, WA area. This page lets our existing customers choose to receive transactional text-message updates about their own restoration job and the related insurance billing. These are service messages, not marketing.

What messages you'll receive

  • Claim review and status updates for your restoration job
  • Payment-forwarding reminders when your insurer issues a payment
  • Past-due / balance reminders for amounts owed under your signed service agreement

How you opt in

During job onboarding, your 911 Restoration project manager will present this consent form on a device, or — if your job is already in progress — we email you a link to this same page. Completing the SMS section is a separate, optional step from your work-authorization / service agreement. Consent to receive texts is not a condition of purchasing or receiving any 911 Restoration service.

Message frequency & cost

Message frequency varies based on activity on your job, claim, and billing. Message and data rates may apply (charged by your mobile carrier, not by 911 Restoration).

How to opt out or get help

Reply STOP to any text to stop messages (one confirmation, no further texts). Reply HELP for help, call 971-533-0214, or email officeassistant@911restoration.com.

Privacy

Your mobile number and SMS consent are used only to operate this 911 Restoration messaging program. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile number or SMS opt-in with any third parties, and we do not use this program for marketing. Full details: Privacy Policy · SMS Terms & Conditions.
